About Me

Harmony Decoder was born from a simple idea: music theory shouldn't feel intimidating. I'm a self-taught musician who spent years feeling lost in the language of chords, scales, and progressions — until things finally clicked. This project is my way of paying that moment forward, offering clear, hands-on tools for players of all levels who want to understand the music they love.

Whether you're picking up a ukulele for the first time or deepening your knowledge of jazz voicings, I'm here to help. My goal is to keep building, keep simplifying, and keep making music theory feel like an adventure rather than a textbook.
